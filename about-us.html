About us and our mission

The Botanical Herbarium of Tbilisi State University was founded immediately after the university was founded in 1918, when the Department of Botany was created.


Along with the development of the Department of Botany, significant botanical material was regularly collected in the TSU Herbarium, which was valuable for both Georgian and foreign scientists. The value of the collection is increased by its diversity, as it includes both typical and rare and endemic exhibits. In parallel with this process, scientific and technical processing, inventory and systematization of the collections were carried out. The fund was replenished with purchased specimens, along with donations from other countries and foreign funds.


The specimens collected by Georgian and foreign botanists include the unique flora of Georgia, Transcaucasia and other regions of the world.


A significant contribution to the replenishment of the herbarium was made by such scientists as: N. Albov, D. Sosnovsky and A. Grossgeim. Also to the botanists working at the university: N. Ketskhoveli, A. Makashvili, R. Gagnidze.


The TSU Herbarium is included in the International List of World Herbariums and also in the International Index of Herbariums (Index Herbariorum) of the New York Botanical Garden.
